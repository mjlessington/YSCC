"YSCC is the intersection where spirituality and diversity collides."

In 2016, a group of people decided to sit down and talk about why the traditional church was no longer a priority in their lives. While we entered the space with zero expectations, we experienced safety, divinity, peace, healing and healthy community while celebrating our similarities and differences alike. It was so good and refreshing that we decided to do it again and again. Our sincere intentions eventually because YSCC, a 501c3 non-profit organization in 2018 that continues to create spaces that allow people to experience healing, wholeness, equity, purpose and inclusion. What we do may seem untraditional and unorthodox to some, yet we continue to witness community happen with strangers who realize they are more alike than different, people are heard and seen, unconscious biases are confronted, and ultimately purpose is aligned. That’s not just what we do, but it is who we are. Because YSCC is at the intersection of spirituality and diversity, we have developed programming and curriculum that helps bridge the gap of community within organizations and corporations. We facilitate small and large group sessions that encourage healing, acceptance, equity and inclusion among other topics. We continue to celebrate milestones individually and collectively as we remain true

What We Do

Because spirituality and diversity are such broad and ever-changing subjects, we would love to assist you in creating curriculum that helps to address the needs of your community and organization. Our research and hands on approach have been proven extremely valuable and successful with other organizations, companies and educational audiences. We assist with evaluating your needs and your audience to help you deliver programming that addresses your expectations in safe and productive spaces.
We have designed sessions that encompass spirituality and diversity that are appropriate for all audiences including youth. Some of our favorites are Intro to Unconscious Bias, Telling Your Story and The Journey to Worthiness. We would love to share with your community and organization while setting safe and conducive space for everyone involved.
We have experienced and professional coaches and pastoral counselors who have dedicated their lives to assisting clients become well, conscious and self-aware on their journey to wholeness. Counseling and coaching sessions that bring spirituality and psychology together in a way that is helpful for the client in hopes of holistic development (large and small groups available). Sessions are available via in-person and virtually. Accommodations made based on requests.

The Conversation

The Conversation is a safe space where we witness the power of dialogue heal, reveal, provoke, engage and establish community. It is indeed the foundation of our organization.

Community Service

YSCC is committed to creating community by assisting our local and regional neighbors with basic human needs. The journey to wholeness begins on the most basic levels of our humanity.

YSCC Retreat

YSCC retreat is dedicated to the care of one’s self and soul. It is an opportunity to practice what we teach as well as develop new and innovative opportunities to meet the needs of our community.
