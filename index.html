What We Do

Programming at YSCC

Development

Because spirituality and diversity are such broad and ever-changing subjects, we would love to assist you in creating curriculum that helps to address the needs of your community and organization. Our research and hands on approach have been proven extremely valuable and successful with other organizations, companies and educational audiences. We assist with evaluating your needs and your audience to help you deliver programming that addresses your expectations in safe and productive spaces.

Workshops and Masterclasses

We offer interactive and engaging sessions that encompass spirituality and diversity and personal growth that are appropriate for all audiences including youth. Some of our favorites are Intro to Unconscious Bias, Telling Your Story,and The Journey to Worthiness. YSCC offers this as a part of our programming suite as well as to other community and corporate organizations. Click here for current and upcoming events.

Soul Care & Coaching

We have experienced and professional coaches and spiritual counselors who have dedicated their lives to assisting clients become well, conscious and self-aware on their journey to wholeness. Counseling and coaching sessions that bring spirituality and psychology together in a way that is helpful for the client in hopes of holistic development (large and small groups available). Sessions are available in-person and virtually. Accommodations made based on requests.

Who We Are

About Your Soul Care Community

  • 2016

    One Conversation

    A group of people decided to sit down and talk about why the traditional church was no longer a priority in their lives. While we entered the space with zero expectations, we experienced safety, divinity, peace, healing and healthy community while celebrating our similarities and differences alike. It was so good and refreshing that we decided to do it again and again.

  • 2018

    It's Official

    Our sincere intentions eventually became Your Soul Care Community, Inc., a 501(c)3 non-profit organization that continues to create spaces that allow people to experience healing, wholeness, equity, purpose and inclusion. What we do may seem untraditional and unorthodox to some, yet we continue to witness community happen with strangers who realize they are more alike than different, people are heard and seen, unconscious biases are confronted, and ultimately purpose is aligned.

  • Today

    Because YSCC is at the intersection of spirituality and diversity, we have developed programming and curriculum that helps bridge the gap of community within organizations and corporations. We facilitate small and large group sessions that encourage healing, acceptance, equity and inclusion among other topics. We continue to celebrate milestones individually and collectively as we remain true to our original intentions of community care, authenticity, wholeness and acceptance!
